About Us

Our mission is to:

  1. Help companies understand the health of their businesses and formulate strategies based on achieveable goals
  2. Help individuals improve their investment choices

Our vision is to develop tools and content that apply insightful business judgement and data science and machine learning techniques onto internal company data and/or the enormous amounts of public data.

Our goals are:

  1. Wherever possible, use open source tools and public data to minimize resource hurdles
  2. Follow reproducible research principles to facilitate discussion and transparency
  3. Reduce the learning curve for data science and machine learning topics by contributing content to the open source community and engaging in discussion
  4. Develop and leverage tools that automate many of the manual processes used to determine relevant KPIs in companies
  5. Leverage business knowledge to ensure goals and strategies are properly set
  6. Develop intelligent tools that adapt to changing market conditions
